About

METO is a managing startup based on connecting you with the professional workers of the METO community that will support you in your minor daily problems like managing a professional photographer or makeup artist or plumber or driver and many more including groceries. Our vision is to reduce the distance between you and professional workers as well as providing our service in your doorstep. It is founded on June 1st, 2020, by some of the compassionate and ambitious professionals to assist various vocations that are an indispensable part of our daily life.
